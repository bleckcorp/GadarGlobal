Privacy
Policy.

This policy explains how Gadar Global Solutions collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our website or contact us about our services.

Last Updated

May 18, 2026

Privacy questions can be sent to hello@gadarsolutions.com.

This Privacy Policy was last updated on May 18, 2026.

1. Introduction

Gadar Global Solutions respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have when you visit our website, contact our team, request a proposal, or work with us.

By using this website or submitting information to us, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you complete a form, send us an email, book a consultation, subscribe to updates, or communicate with us about a project.

  • Contact information, such as your name, email address, phone number, company name, and role.
  • Project information, such as your service interests, budget range, goals, timelines, and business requirements.
  • Communication information, including messages, attachments, notes, and records of our correspondence with you.
  • Website usage information, such as pages visited, referring URLs, device type, browser type, approximate location, IP address, and interaction data.

3. How We Use Information

We use personal information to operate our website, respond to enquiries, provide services, improve our work, and maintain secure business operations.

  • Respond to messages, consultation requests, proposals, and support needs.
  • Understand your business goals and recommend suitable AI, automation, software, web, mobile, or enterprise solutions.
  • Prepare estimates, statements of work, contracts, invoices, and project communications.
  • Improve our website, content, services, user experience, and internal processes.
  • Send service updates, administrative messages, or marketing communications where permitted.
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, security incidents, misuse, or unlawful activity.
  • Comply with legal, tax, accounting, regulatory, and contractual obligations.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies, pixels, analytics tools, server logs, and similar technologies to remember preferences, understand website traffic, measure performance, and improve the visitor experience.

You can usually control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how parts of the website function.

5. Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable data protection laws require a legal basis, we process personal information based on one or more of the following grounds: your consent, performance of a contract, steps taken before entering a contract, legitimate business interests, compliance with legal obligations, or protection of rights and security.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when needed to operate our business, provide services, protect rights, or comply with the law.

  • Service providers that help us host the website, manage communications, process forms, analyse website performance, deliver projects, or handle business operations.
  • Professional advisers, including lawyers, accountants, auditors, insurers, and consultants.
  • Authorities, regulators, courts, or other parties when disclosure is required by law or needed to protect rights, safety, security, or property.
  • Business successors if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, restructuring, asset sale, or similar transaction.

7. International Transfers

Because we serve clients across regions and may use cloud-based service providers, your information may be processed outside your country of residence. Where required, we use reasonable safeguards intended to protect personal information during international transfers.

8. Data Retention

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including to respond to enquiries, provide services, maintain business records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations.

When information is no longer needed, we will delete, anonymise, or securely archive it where appropriate.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical, administrative, and organisational measures designed to protect personal information against unauthorised access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. No website, email system, or internet transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, update, delete, restrict, object to, or receive a copy of your personal information. You may also have the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To make a privacy request, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

11. Marketing Communications

If you receive marketing emails from us, you can opt out by following the unsubscribe instructions in the email or by contacting us directly. We may still send non-marketing messages, such as service, transactional, legal, or security communications.

12. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party websites, platforms, or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing personal information with them.

13. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for business users and are not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or business practices. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
